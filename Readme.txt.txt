Game Name: Doomion: Requiem of a Nightmare
Team Name: Alt F4
Team Members: Caleb, Abishek, Sule, Derek

Game Instructions:
Doomion: Requiem of a Nightmare is a horror exploration game. In this first playable, the player begins outside the Student Union at night and must find a way inside.

Controls:
- WASD: Move
- Mouse: Interact with clickable items and buttons
- E: Interact with nearby objects such as tables and doors
- Left Click: Pick up items in table view
- F: Activate flashlight
- S: Start the game from the title screen
- R: Restart from the death screen

How to Play:
- The game begins at the title screen. Press the Start button or S to begin.
- The player starts outside in a dark area.
- A bat enemy patrols the path and can attack the player.
- The bat uses spatial sound, so its chirping helps the player figure out where it is located.
- The bat is invisible in the darkness unless the player shines the flashlight on it.
- Move carefully toward the tables while avoiding the bat.
- Approach a table and press E to enter the table interaction view.
- In table view, click on an item to add it to your inventory.
- Use the Back button to leave table view.
- The table on the right contains the flashlight and batteries.
- The table on the left contains the hammer and batteries.
- The flashlight does not take up inventory space.
- If the bat attacks and kills the player, a death screen appears. Press the Reset button or R to restart.
- After getting the hammer, use it to break the glass door and enter the Student Union.
- The first playable ends after the player breaks the door and reaches the “To Be Continued” screen.

Game Walkthrough:
1. Start at the title screen and press the Start button or S.
2. Spawn outside the Student Union in darkness.
3. Listen for the bat’s chirping to determine where it is.
4. Go to the right table and press E to interact.
5. Click the flashlight to collect it.
6. Click the batteries on the table to collect them.
7. Use the Back button to exit table view.
8. Use the flashlight to reveal the otherwise invisible bat and avoid it.
9. Go to the left table and press E to interact.
10. Click the hammer to collect it.
11. Click the batteries on the table to collect them.
12. Use the Back button to exit table view.
13. Move up to the glass entrance door of the Student Union.
14. Use the hammer to break the glass door.
15. Enter the Student Union to trigger the end of the first playable.
16. If you die, press the Reset button or R on the death screen to restart.

Cheat Codes:
- Ctrl + Shift + F: Use the flashlight without having it.

Advanced GameMaker Features:
- Paths
  The bat enemy uses pathing to patrol the outside area.
- Spatial Sound
  The bat’s chirping uses spatial audio so the player can hear its location and use sound to avoid danger.
