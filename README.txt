Game Name: Doomion: Requiem of a Nightmare
Team Name: Alt F4
Team Members: Caleb, Abishek, Sule, Derek


Game Instructions:
Doomion: Requiem of a Nightmare is a horror exploration game where the player controls a wizard trying to recover his stolen staff from a corrupted version of the Student Union. The player begins outside the Student Union at night and must work floor by floor through enemies, locked rooms, hidden items, and environmental obstacles to reach the staff and escape through a portal.


Controls:
- WASD: Move
- Mouse: Interact with clickable items and buttons
- E: Interact with nearby objects such as tables, cupboards, doors, elevators, and stairs
- Left Click: Pick up items in table/cupboard view
- F: Activate or deactivate flashlight
- S: Start the game from the title screen
- L: Shows Leaderboard from title screen
- Q: Quit from title screen or pause screen
- R: Restart from the death screen
- Space: Close text boxes/notes when prompted
- F11: Fullscreen
- Escape: Go back from table, pause, or note screens


How to Play:
- The game begins at the title screen. Press the Start button or S to begin.
- The player starts outside the Student Union in darkness.
- A bat enemy patrols the outside path and can attack the player.
- The bat uses spatial sound, so its chirping helps the player determine its location.
- The bat is invisible in the darkness unless the player shines the flashlight on it.
- The flashlight can also be used to ward off the bat, causing it to move away from the player while caught in the beam.
- Approach tables, cupboards, doors, stairs, or the elevator and press E to interact.
- In table/cupboard view, click on an item to add it to your inventory.
- Use the Back button to leave table/cupboard view.
- Batteries refill the flashlight.
- Food items such as chips and burgers restore health.
- If food would heal above the player’s maximum health, the extra healing is converted into score.
- The player’s score starts high and decreases over time.
- The score does not decrease while the game is paused.
- The final score is saved when the player enters the portal at the end of the game.
- The leaderboard button on the title screen shows past scores.
- If enemies reduce the player’s health to zero, a death screen appears. Press the Reset button or R to restart.


Game Walkthrough:


1. Title Screen / Start
- Start at the title screen.
- Press the Start button or S to begin.
- Optional: use the Instructions button to view the controls.
- Optional: use the Leaderboard button to view previous scores.


2. Outside the Student Union
- Spawn outside the Student Union in darkness.
- Listen for the bat’s chirping to determine where it is.
- Move carefully toward the tables while avoiding the bat.
- Interact with the tables by pressing E.
- Collect the flashlight, batteries, hammer, food, and notes.
- Use F to turn the flashlight on or off.
- Shine the flashlight toward the bat to reveal it and push it away.
- Use the hammer to break the glass entrance door.
- Enter the Student Union.


3. Floor 1
- On Floor 1, make your way left through the Starbucks and Barnes & Noble area.
- A bat continues to threaten the player, so use the flashlight to reveal it and keep it away.
- Search tables and cupboards for items, food, batteries, and notes.
- Move toward Wendy’s in the top-left area (red floor).
- Find the key on the table.
- Use the key to unlock the Bojangles door.
- Inside Bojangles (top right + yellow floor), search the cupboard to find the fuse.
- Bring the fuse to the elevator (Left Wall).
- Use the fuse to repair the elevator.
- Enter the elevator to reach Floor 2.


4. Floor 2
- Floor 2 introduces a crocodile along with the bat.
- The crocodile patrols the floor and can chase the player if they get too close.
- Avoid the crocodile while continuing to use the flashlight to deal with the bat.
- Search the rooms and collect supplies as needed.
- Go to the top-right room (green floor) and collect the key.
- Use the key to access the bottom-left room.
- Search the bottom-left room (green floor) and collect the ID card.
- Use the ID card to open the ID-locked door.
- Go up the stairs to reach Floor 3.


5. Floor 3
- Floor 3 includes the Florida Man, the crocodile, and the bat.
- The player must avoid all three enemies while searching for the staff.
- The bat can still be pushed away with the flashlight.
- The crocodile and Florida Man patrol the area and chase the player when close.
- Go to the bottom-right room and search the table for a key.
- Use that key to enter the top-right room.
- Move left through the floor while avoiding enemies.
- Find the staff on the table.
- Pick up the staff to open the portal behind the player.
- Enter the portal to complete the game.


6. Ending
- After entering the portal, the score stops decreasing.
- The final score is saved to the leaderboard.
- The epilogue plays.
- After the epilogue, the game shows the final ending screen.

Advanced GameMaker Features:
- Paths
  Enemies use paths to patrol different areas of the Student Union. The bat, crocodile, and Florida Man follow patrol routes but can change behavior when the wizard gets close.


- Spatial Sound
  The enemies use spatial audio for their sounds. The player can listen to the direction and volume of the chirps to determine where the enemy is located. This helps with the bat which can be hidden before shining a light on it.


- Shaders
  The game uses shaders in multiple gameplay-related ways. Cheat mode uses shaders to visually show infinite health and infinite battery. The wizard also uses shader effects when taking damage, healing, and gaining score from overhealing.


- Views
  The game uses a viewport and camera system to follow and zoom in on the wizard as the player explores the Student Union.


- Object Inheritance
  Object inheritance is used throughout the project. Buttons share common button-style behavior, 3D pickup objects use shared item interaction behavior, and cupboard/table interactions reuse related functionality.


- Smart Opponents / NPC AI
  Enemies such as the bat, crocodile, and Florida Man use patrol behavior and react to the wizard when he gets close. The bat has additional behavior where it moves away from the player when caught in the flashlight beam.


Scoring:
- The player starts with a maximum score.
- The score decreases over time during active gameplay.
- The score does not decrease while paused or while viewing table/cupboard rooms.
- Batteries can add score based on the remaining flashlight battery value before refilling.
- Food restores health.
- If food would heal the player above maximum health, the extra healing becomes bonus score.
- The final score is recorded when the player enters the portal.
- Past scores can be viewed from the Leaderboard button on the title screen.
